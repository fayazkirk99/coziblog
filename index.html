11 MAY 2026

I had just moved from Lilongwe to Blantyre to live with my grandmother and proceed in my ambitious pursuit of formal education as a computing student.It was about 9 hours and possibly 30 or maybe 45 minutes into a Wednesday morning when i was pacing through the streets of Blantyre's Town.My forehead was glistening from sweat not due to the excessive amount of lotion i applied on my brown face earlier in my attempt to keep up with the day's schedule,instead the culprit responsible was the relentless blazing sun that came out to greet each malawian on his way to adhere to our incompetent capitalistic economy.My hair was being kept steady at the back of my head by these black affordable headphones, holding my ears hostage as 808s blast modern trap music at the highest possible volume,but instead of my ears bleeding my body was receiving excessive amounts of energy that kept me pacing.I had taken a countable amount of steps pass the bus-stop aiming for the school campus when i noticed a group of girls turn the corner while chatting,exchanging laughs with every 2 steps they each took.i could also notice that they had a schedule to attend to as well.One of the girls was dressed in muslim attire and within this short moment my eyes could not help but to throw a tiny gaze towards her inviting face.A tiny gaze that turned out to seem infinite because within those few seconds,she was the most beautiful girl in the world. My mind is sort of convinced that she exchanged a gaze with me but i half believe it could be my ego luring my brain into a pool of delusion.Theres something peculiar about seeing something beautiful whether it be a woman or other majestic objects of nature that just,in the simplest of words,keeps a guy like me going.I did not dwell in the moment for too long in the present for class was where i ought to be.I quickly shuffled through the strrets walking pass vendors in the same perimeter,each vendor had infront of them a layout of familiar products u could get from the next vendor for a similar if not identical price.I grabbed a piece of candy to sweeten the taste of my breath while listening to what seemed to be the final song to escape my headphones and into my ears before i reached the main door to my supposed class.I was the first one to be present,so much for early birds and catching worms.

some people become part of your internal world even when they are absent.
